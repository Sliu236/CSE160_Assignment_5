How to?

Mouse Wheel - Zoom in/out

Right Mouse Button - drag camera

Left Mouse Button - Enable FPS mode

WASD - move the camera

Mouse - rotate the camera

1 - Free view mode

2 - Gunner view

3 - Driver view

4 - Pilot view

PressESC Disable FPS mode

When you first enter the interface, please wait about 20 seconds for the shader to finish loading the model.

Extra implementated features:

1. Shadows

2. Fog

3. Multiple camera views
